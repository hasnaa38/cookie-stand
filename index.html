Pat's Salmon Cookies

Our Story

One night in Seattle, Pat Benson; the author of Professional Baking, has come up with an idea by combining two signature Seattle icons: Pat has developed a recipe for a coffee-time confection called Salmon Cookies. These cookies made into the shape of a salmon that has just a hint of fishy goodness.
Later on, he decided to take his idea and turn it a business. Today, Pat's Salmon Cookies became a well known cookies store located in five different cities around the world.

Merch

As a company, we believe its our responsibility and mission to show our support the Sea Change campaign by World Animal Protection. The campaign reduces the huge suffering caused by ‘ghost gear’ – abandoned fishing gear that turns oceans into death traps for sea animals. Their goal is to protect sea life and move towards a future free from the ghost fishing gear threat
You can participate in the campaign too by getting our store's merch, all the raised money will be donated to World Animal Protection.
